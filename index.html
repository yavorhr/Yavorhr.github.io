About me

Hello and welcome to my page! Allow me to introduce myself briefly — I am a graduate in Finance and Accounting, and my professional career has been built and continues to grow in these fields. From 2015 to 2019 I worked in the financial department of Ingram Micro SSC EMEA. or the past six years, I have been working at PPD Bulgaria Ltd as part of the Accounts Payable department, supporting clinical research.
